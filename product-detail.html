The Thermamite digital catering thermometer offers accuracy, flexibility and ease of use, coupled with the durability and reliability required by the food and catering industry in helping to meet today’s food hygiene legislation. The Thermamite thermometer incorporates a clear, easy to read LCD display with a precise read-out of temperature over the range of -50 to 300°C with a 1 °C resolution. Low battery and open circuit indication are also displayed, when applicable. The unit will power off automatically after ten minutes, maximising battery life. The Thermamite is housed in an ergonomic, tough ABS case that includes ‘Biomaster’ additive that reduces bacterial growth. Each Thermamite thermometer is supplied with a permanently attached pointed, stainless steel food penetration probe (Ø3.3 x 100 mm) and a 500 mm PVC lead. What is Biomaster? Biomaster additives are powerful broad-spectrum inorganic, silver-based biocides for use in plastics, helping to reduce hygiene risks. The concentration of silver, in the patented anti-microbial active agent in Biomaster, provides a release of silver ions on demand, which in turn safely inhibits microbial growth.
